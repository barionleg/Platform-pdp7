## About
**CPCE** is a **Freeware - Non-Commercial** emulator for **Amstrad CPC** made by **Cesar Nicolas Gonzalez**

This emulator does **not** specificly needs a BIOS image to run!

_CPCE is an Amstrad CPC emulator._
