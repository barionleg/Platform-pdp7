## About
**Horizon** is a **GNU GPLv2** emulator for **Acorn BBC** made by **Chris Lam**

This emulator **needs a BIOS image** to run!

_Horizon emulates the Acorn BBC Micro (Model B) with 32K RAM. B+ and_
